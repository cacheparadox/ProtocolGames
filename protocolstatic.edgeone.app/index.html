PROTOCOL STATIC

Recover the corrupted binary image by following the row and column signatures.

  • The numbers indicate unbroken blocks of filled cells in that row or column.
  • Multiple numbers mean there is at least one empty space between the blocks.
  • Use FILL mode to restore cells, and MARK (✕) for empty space.
  • Clues turn green when a row/column is satisfied.
